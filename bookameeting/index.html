Book a meeting

Fill in this questionnaire to book a meeting with the digital identity team.

Please answer all the questions as best as you can.

Don’t worry about getting this questionnaire exactly right. It just helps us prepare and makes sure we have the right people at the meeting.


What does your service do?

In 1 to 2 sentences, what does your service do for the user? (For example, ‘Our service will help students enroll in study’.)


Why do you need digital identity?

What best describes your trigger for looking into digital identity?





Who are your users?

Who are the users of your service? (For example, students, business owners).


How many users?

Roughly how many transactions will the service have in a year?


What are your timeframes?

When do you need to onboard?


How is your service funded?

How is your service currently funded, or how are you planning to fund it?


Which level of assurance?

What is the highest level of identity proofing you need for a person to use the service?



There is guidance on the levels of assurance (this link will open a new tab so you do not lose your place).
